      About the Event

      Where Robotics Meets
      Industrial Intelligence

      IEEE RAS × IAS Hackathon 2026 is a collaborative 36-hour engineering challenge that unites students passionate about robotics, automation, and intelligent systems. Hosted jointly by the IEEE Robotics & Automation Society (RAS) and the IEEE Industry Applications Society (IAS) student chapters at MITS Gwalior, this event pushes participants to design, prototype, and pitch real-world solutions.

      Bring your ideas, your curiosity, and your team — and leave with industry connections, hands-on experience, and possibly a winning project that solves a genuine engineering challenge.

      "Bridging the gap between academic innovation and industrial impact."
